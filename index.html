about me

My name is Herbert Brown. I also go by "Satch". Among my various titles, I'm an instructor for the Mississippi Coding Academies. While not a "traditional" developer (if that is such a thing), I've devoted my day hours to not only teaching, but more importantly, learning as much as I can about programming. It has grown into an insatiable passion of mine as I continue to grow in this study.

Python is my entry-point language into the programming world. I am also learning HTML/CSS, JavaScript, SQL, & PHP, as well as the library React. Basically, the journey of learning programming is, in itself, a reward.

When I don't have my nose stuck in the computer, I enjoy DJ'ing, collecting vinyl, reading, working out, and spending time with my wife and two children.
